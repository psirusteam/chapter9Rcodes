CHAPTER 9 - EXAMPLES IN R

2.1 1. Introduction

The purpose of this document is to analyze and generate valid statistical inferences using survey data from the Ethiopia Socioeconomic Survey (ESS), conducted in collaboration with the Central Statistics Agency of Ethiopia (CSA) and the World Bank.

The analysis includes: - Cleaning and loading data. - Exploring sampled enumeration areas and households. - Defining a survey design for valid statistical inference.
